Experience

Squadrn.com Beta

Squadrn is a web app for the aviation industry that simplifies the management of material, quotes, sales, and contacts all in one place.

Squadrn uses Heroku, Ruby on Rails, Devise, Sunspot Solr, LESS, jQuery, SendGrid, and New Relic.

Vitals.com

Vitals.com is a ratings and reviews site for doctors and dentists. It serves nearly 14 million page views per month according to Quantcast.com.

Vitals uses Lithium, Solarium Solr, LESS, jQuery, and New Relic.

Vitals.com Micro Machine

The 'Micro Machine' was a micro site generator. It was built as an admin tool to easily create sites for providers under a distict domain.

Micro Machine uses Slim, LESS, and jQuery.

Victory.com

Victory Christian Center is a church in Tulsa, OK. It has around seventeen thousand members. They provide live streaming of their services, a media archive of their weekly television show, and many other resources for the church.

Victory used CodeIgniter, jQuery, Flow Player, Akamai, and Bluefin Payment Gateway.

Vbitulsa.com Registration

VBI Tulsa is the bible school associated with Victory Christian Center. Vbitulsa.com Registration was a web app that allowed the faculty to manage what courses were offered on a semester basis. It also allowed student to create an account, select the classes they wanted for either part-time or full-time, make tuition payments, as well as other many other school needs.

Vbitulsa.com used PHP and Bluefin Payment Gateway.
